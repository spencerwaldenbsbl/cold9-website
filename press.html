New Venture Expo Showcases Student and Incubator Businesses

Cold9 took 1st place in the “prototype” category at the University of Tampa’s New Venture Expo, earning $3,000 to help bring the first insulated baseball pant to market. Founders Spencer Walden ’29, Sam Levine ’29, and Joshua Greenspon ’28 were among 73 ventures featured at the annual event in the Lowth Entrepreneurship Center.
