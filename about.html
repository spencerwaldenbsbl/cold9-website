02 · The Problem

We Felt the Cold.
So We Fixed It.

Every baseball player has been there — shivering through an early-spring opener, a late-fall doubleheader, a winter showcase where your breath fogs the lineup card and your fingers go numb on the grip.

And the “solutions” suck: thermal underwear that bunches up and kills your range of motion, bulky compression layers that look unprofessional under the uniform, or the classic — grit your teeth and freeze.

03 · The Solution

One Pant.
Zero Layers.

Cold9 is the first-ever baseball pant engineered with an integrated thermal lining. No extra layers. No bulk. Just one pant that keeps you warm and lets you play at your best.

  • Outer ShellPro-grade 100% polyester
  • Inner LinerSoft double-brushed fleece
  • Impact ZonesNylon-spandex barrier at knees and seat for slide protection
  • FitIndistinguishable from a premium baseball pant — the insulation is invisible from the outside
