Waist

Wrap a soft tape around the natural waistline (where you’d normally wear a belt). Keep it snug but not tight.

Inseam

Measure from the inside top of the leg down to where you want the pant to break (typically just above the cleat).

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of the hips, usually 7–9 inches below the natural waist. Keep the tape level.
